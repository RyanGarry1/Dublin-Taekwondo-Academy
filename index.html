Welcome to Dublin Taekwondo Academy

Dublin Taekwondo Academy was established in 1996 by Head instructor Master Ryan Garry It is the longest running Taekwondo club in Ireland and the first to be offically recognized by the Irish Martial Arts Commission (IMAC) the national governing body of martial arts in Ireland. We teach Olympic style Taekwondo derived from the World Taekwondo Federation (WTF) syllabus and all of our instructors are Kukkiwon certified Black Belts



Our Mission

Here at Dublin Taekwondo Academy we offer a host of benefits to our members We hold 4 National tournaments per calendar year, where both Kyrougi & Poomsae is represented we offer 4 yearly gradings, including 1 black belt only grading, hosted by our guest Kukkiwon certified Grand Master. We also offer our students personalised training programs, extra curricular classes based on their choice of discipline and private 1-to-1 training sessions


Taekwondo for all ages

Taekwondo is an excellent form of physical activity for children and for people of all ages. Taekwondo helps people to develop balance, co-ordination, agility and speed. It also improves movement quality, cardiovascular health and joint and muscle flexibility which we believe is just as an important for our adult students to enjoy a higher quality of life outside of the Dojang and helps the long term athletic development of our younger students.
